My name is Eduardo da Silva and I am Portuguese. I am a senior software engineer/architect/tech-lead with a solid academic background (PhD in Computer Science) and hands-on experience on designing and building software products. I am a team player/builder with a keen eye on continuous learning/experimentation, so that I can design and build products as best as possible (= striving for excellence while keeping a sharp pragmatic eye on results).

I have also been founder and lead engineer of a startup company.
