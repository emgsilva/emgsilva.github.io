My name is Eduardo da Silva. I am a senior software engineer/architect/tech-lead with a solid academic background (PhD in Computer Science) and hands-on experience on designing and building software products. I am doing this on the context of teams building data science empowered products (not only teams with data scientists, but the full stack required to build a product: data, backend, front-end, experimentation, etc).
