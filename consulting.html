Sociotechnical Architecture Assessment & Consulting

Since 2018 I been working and researching on the topic of Sociotehcnical Architecture and pioneering ideas and mechanisms to implement it in modern tech-enabled organizations, especially on Product-led organizations.

What is Sociotechnical Architecture (click for more details)

What is "Sociotechnical Architecture"? My definition: "Sociotechnical Architecture is about taking an holistic co-design approach to technical and organizational systems, given the inherent impact they have on each other." To me this is the next level of evolution for how we approach building tech-enabled products, i.e.: stop working in partial optimizations and start building and evolving the teams and technical architectures of the products they build with an aligned understanding. Important: this is a continuous activity, i.e.: you need to create operating models that embrace and thrive on that (this is not a project that finishes after one year).

For more details on my work check my Sociotechnical Architecture and Systems page and my talks page, as I regularly write and talk on this subject.


Typical Activities:
  • Sociotechnical Architecture introduction and coaching - help introducing Sociotechnical Architecture thinking in your organization.
  • Sociotechnical Architecture assessment and consulting - help you assess and understand your current Sociotechnical Architecture and how to define evolution strategies.
  • Sociotechnical Architecture as enabler of Product-led organizations - position Sociotechnical Architecture thinking in product-led organizations (where you need to setup the right sociotechnical systems to truly achieve Product Thinking goals).
